About ShowGirl Temple

In ShowGirl Temple we dance in worship of our sexy selves, honor and connect with all aspects of femininity -- ferocity, frustration, fear, joy, determination, sweetness, sexuality. We break free of shame and step into the most glittering manifestations of ourselves, shining our light for all, but most importantly for ourselves.

Tended by High Priestess of Tease, Burlesque Performer, Producer and MC, Eva D'Luscious, ShowGirl Temple offers body positive Performance Workshops, Burlesque and act polishing classes based in dance technique from Eva's many years of study and stage performance. Book a session online or in the ShowGirl Temple, Portland, OR.

Eva also is available for private classes, performance and speaking engagements.

Share the journey with us on Facebook or join the email list to receive updates on class and workshop dates, the release of online videos and more!

Bling & Blessings! Eva D'Luscious
