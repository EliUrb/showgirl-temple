About ShowGirl Temple

In ShowGirl Temple we dance in worship of our sexy selves, honor and connect with all aspects of femininity -- ferocity, frustration, fear, joy, determination, sweetness, sexuality. We break free of shame and step into the most glittering manifestations of ourselves, shining our light for all, but most importantly for ourselves.

Tended by High Priestess of Tease, Burlesque Performer, Producer and MC, Eva D'Luscious, ShowGirl Temple offers body positive Performance Workshops, Burlesque and act polishing classes based in dance technique from Eva's many years of study and stage performance. Book a session online or in the ShowGirl Temple, Portland, OR.

Eva also is available for private classes, performance and speaking engagements.

All about Eva

Eva D’Luscious is a Burlesque performer, producer, MC and instructor based in Portland, OR. She is High-Priestess of Tease in the Showgirl Temple, bringing Goddesses and Legendary Women to Burlesque stages.

Founder and Creative Director of Cabaret de Caliente, Eva produces Burlesque shows in the Pacific Northwest and Northern California, including Whole Lotta Love Burlesque Tribute to Led Zeppelin, and StripCity Burlesque, a strip back in time to live music. In 2016 the Russian River Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence named Eva "Saint Bublicious" in appreciation for her fundraising work for the nonprofit organization.

Eva regularly MCs and performs at Papermoon Cabaret, Portland's Classic Burlesque Showcase, and has performed at Tease-O-Rama, Hubba Hubba Revue, SinFerno, Naughty Bit Burlesque, Pink Lady DiAnn's Cat's Meow, The Edwardian Ball, Edmonton Burlesque Festival, Great Girlesque Festival Toronto, Kitty Nights, Tiki Oasis, Oregon Burlesque Festival, Tiki Kon, The Snake Oil Festival, GEAR Con PDX, Trapeze, Burning Man, Masquerotica, Little Minsky’s, New Bohemia New Year's Eve, Baxtolo Drom and many other saucy stages.

Eva D'Luscious is available to perform Burlesque, Fan and Cabaret dance at private events and shows, to MC, and for teaching Burlesque & Showgirl Dance Technique.
